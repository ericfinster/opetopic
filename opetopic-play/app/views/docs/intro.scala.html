Introduction

Opetopic is a higher categorical proof assistant.

While on the one hand, one can seen the project of building such a thing as a purely technological undertaking, there is also a certain philosohpical background which motivates such a project.

From a mathematician's perspective, the project of building a proof assistant of any sort is often view as a kind of technological undertaking independent of the doing mathematics. The advent of Univalent Type Theory, however, has placed this subject square in the modern search for a reasonable theory of higher categories, and it is with this motivation in mind that I built Opetopic.

The theory of higher categories is playing a more and more crucial role in modern mathematics and physics. The cobordism hypothesis relates higher categoies and the theory of manifolds and surgery. Topological Quantum Fields Theories place higher categories as the heart of modern phyical inquries. The study of higher anologs of rings, and more generally the issue of coherence is an intergral part of modern algebraic topology and algebraic geometry.
