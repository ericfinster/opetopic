Opetopic Diagrams II

We being now with an actual opetopic diagram:

The diagram consists of a squence of nestings, each in a zoom relation with the next. The nestings are ordered by dimension, starting from zero, so that the diagram above shows a five dimensional opetope. Moreover, each box corresponds to a face or cell of the whole five dimensional structure.

The four boxes in the first nesting, for example, correspond to four zero dimensional faces. That is, this five dimensional opetope has four vertices.
